"Here, Null-ren! Happy birthday!"

Null stared at Chisa, then at the huge ice cream she was pushing toward them. Strawberry and banana, even though they couldn't remember when they'd mentioned their preference in front of Chisa.

"Take it before it melts," Chisa insisted.

Null obeyed.

"Thank you, but... how did you even get it?"
"I bought it," Chisa said, her expression daring them to object to that.
"But... you don't have money?" Null asked, feeling more and more lost.
